## Restrictions & Excluded Classes

While we have a broad appetite, the following exposures are not eligible:

- Logging, recycling, tire shredding, scrap metals operations
- Cotton pickers, saw mills
- Underground drills over 10"
- Severe wildfire exposures
- 100% unscheduled equipment (must combine with scheduled)
- Oil & gas exposures
- Mid-term builders risk (admitted product)
- PC 9/10 construction
- Difficult agriculture equipment (pivots)
- New venture motor truck cargo
- More than 20 power units
- Household goods (movers & storage)
- Barrier islands or less than 1 mile to tidal water (except X-Wind non-admitted product)

## Non-Admitted Option Available

For risks that don't fit our admitted program, we offer a **non-admitted E&S product** with the following features:

- Contractors Equipment, Builders Risk, Installation Floater, Scheduled Property Floaters
- **$2.5 million TIV maximum**
- **Mid-term Builders Risk available** (must have coverage since project inception for X-wind in tier 1 & 2)
- Equipment leased, rented, or borrowed from others
- Flexible coverage form
