## Program Guidelines

<div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-8 my-12">
  <div class="space-y-4">
    <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-4 text-slate-900 dark:text-white">Target Appetite</h3>
    <div class="prose prose-slate dark:prose-invert">

**Ideal Contractors:**
- Trade contractors (electrical, plumbing, HVAC, etc.)
- Small remodelers and renovation contractors
- Specialty trade contractors
- Artisan contractors
- Target risks under $5M in receipts

**Coverage Features:**
- Flexible coverage form
- Up to 15 new residential starts per year allowed
- Work up to 6 feet below grade considered
- Subcontractor exposures with proper risk transfer (copy of sub agreement required)

</div>
</div>

<div class="space-y-4">
<h3 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-4 text-slate-900 dark:text-white">What We Don't Cover</h3>
<div class="prose prose-slate dark:prose-invert">

**Restrictions:**
- Not a market for difficult trades (Roofing, Pile Driving, Shoring, Underpinning)
- No oil/gas or Marine exposures
- Not suitable for new ventures with 100% subcontracted exposures
- Gross receipts must be under $5M

**Available Endorsements:**
- Waiver of Subrogation (WOS)
- Primary Non-Contributory (PNC)
- Several different Additional Insured forms

</div>
