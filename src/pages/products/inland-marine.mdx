## Coverage Types

### Bailees Customers Insurance

Protection for businesses that have temporary custody of customer property, including repair shops, service providers, and storage facilities.

**Typical Classes:**
- Auto repair and body shops
- Electronics repair facilities
- Dry cleaners and laundromats
- Storage facilities
- Equipment rental companies

### Scheduled Property Floater

All-risk coverage for specifically scheduled high-value items that move between locations.

**Common Uses:**
- Medical equipment
- Audio/visual equipment
- Computer servers and IT equipment
- Scientific instruments
- Survey equipment

### Electronic Equipment

Comprehensive coverage for electronic data processing equipment, including computers, servers, telecommunications equipment, and related peripherals.

**Coverage Features:**
- All-risk coverage including mechanical breakdown
- Extra expense coverage
- Valuable papers and records
- Off-premises coverage

### Medical Equipment

Specialized coverage for portable medical equipment used by healthcare providers, mobile clinics, and diagnostic services.

**Examples:**
- Portable X-ray machines
- Ultrasound equipment
- Diagnostic tools
- Medical lasers
- Mobile dental equipment

### Builders Risk

Project-based coverage for buildings under construction, renovation, or remodeling.

**Coverage Includes:**
- Building materials on-site
- Permanently installed fixtures
- Temporary structures
- Architect and engineering fees

**Typical Projects:**
- New construction ($[AMOUNT] - $[AMOUNT])
- Renovations and remodels
- Tenant improvements
- Light commercial projects

### Contractors Equipment

All-risk coverage for contractor-owned tools, machinery, and equipment used in construction and related trades.

**Covered Equipment:**
- Heavy equipment (excavators, bulldozers, loaders)
- Power tools and hand tools
- Scaffolding and ladders
- Small machinery and generators
- Trailers and non-licensed vehicles
