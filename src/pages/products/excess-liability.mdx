## Key Coverage Features

### True Follow Form
Our excess policy follows the same terms, conditions, and exclusions as your underlying primary policies—providing seamless additional protection.

### Defense Costs
Defense costs are covered in addition to policy limits, providing full protection when underlying limits are exhausted.

### Drop Down Coverage
If the underlying policy is canceled or non-renewed, our excess policy drops down to provide primary coverage (subject to the underlying retention).

### Broad Named Insured
Automatic coverage for subsidiaries, newly acquired entities, and designated additional insureds as shown in the underlying policies.

## Excluded Classes

We do not provide excess liability coverage for:

- Asbestos operations
- Lead paint operations
- Hazardous waste disposal
- Bars and nightclubs
- Adult entertainment
- Amusement parks
- Aviation operations (other than incidental)
- Underground operations
- Residential habitational (apartments, condos)
