## Coverage Types

### Building Coverage
Protection for the building structure, including:
- Permanent fixtures and fittings
- HVAC systems
- Plumbing and electrical systems
- Additions and extensions
- Outdoor fixtures (fences, signs, etc.)

### Business Personal Property (BPP)
Coverage for contents and equipment, including:
- Furniture and fixtures
- Machinery and equipment
- Inventory and stock
- Supplies and materials
- Improvements and betterments (for tenants)

### Additional Coverages (Included)
- Debris removal
- Fire department service charges
- Pollutant cleanup and removal
- Preservation of property
- Fire extinguisher recharge
- Business income (available as endorsement)
- Extra expense (available as endorsement)

## Catastrophe Coverage

We provide coverage in catastrophe-exposed areas including:

### Wind/Hurricane
- Coastal tier 1 and tier 2 territories
- Separate wind deductibles apply (typically 2-5%)
- Named storm deductibles where required by state

### Earthquake
- Coverage available in seismic zones
- Separate earthquake deductibles apply (typically 5-10%)
- Coverage subject to appropriate construction and protective measures

### Flood (Limited)
- Limited flood coverage available outside SFHA zones
- Sublimits and separate deductibles apply
- NFIP or private flood coverage recommended for SFHA properties

## Valuation Options

### Replacement Cost
Coverage pays to replace damaged property with new property of like kind and quality, without deduction for depreciation.

### Actual Cash Value
Coverage pays replacement cost less depreciation. Lower premium option for older buildings or specific situations.
