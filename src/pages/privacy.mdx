## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Request a quote or apply for insurance coverage
- Create an account on our platform
- Contact us with inquiries or support requests
- Subscribe to our newsletter or marketing communications
- Participate in surveys or promotions

**Types of personal information we may collect include:**
- Name and contact information (email, phone number, mailing address)
- Date of birth and Social Security Number (for underwriting purposes)
- Business information (company name, EIN, business type, revenue)
- Property information (addresses, building details, equipment lists)
- Financial information (payment details, bank account information)
- Insurance history (prior coverage, loss runs, claims history)
- Driver information (license numbers, driving records)

### Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity:
- IP address and location data
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages viewed and time spent on pages
- Referring website addresses
- Date and time of visits
- Cookie data and similar tracking technologies

### Information from Third Parties
We may receive information about you from third parties, including:
- Insurance agents and brokers
- Credit reporting agencies
- Public records databases
- Loss history databases (ISO, LexisNexis, etc.)
- Motor vehicle records
- Previous insurance carriers

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activity and improve your experience.

### Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies**
- Required for website functionality
- Remember your login session
- Enable core features and security

**Analytics Cookies**
- Track website usage and performance
- Analyze user behavior and trends
- Identify technical issues

**Functional Cookies**
- Remember your preferences
- Personalize your experience
- Enable enhanced features

**Marketing Cookies**
- Track marketing campaign effectiveness
- Deliver targeted advertising
- Measure conversion rates

### Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
- View and delete cookies
- Block third-party cookies
- Block all cookies
- Receive notifications when cookies are set

Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

### Security Measures Include:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest (TLS/SSL)
- Secure data centers with physical access controls
