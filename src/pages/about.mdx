## Our Story

<div class="prose prose-lg dark:prose-invert max-w-none">

Gridiron Insurance Underwriters was founded in 2006. Headquartered in Plantation-Florida, we are a dedicated Program Manager and MGU.

Gridiron is strategically placed to manage insurance facilities and affinity based program business on a regional and national basis. 

With a disciplined and consistent underwriting approach, we are committed to creating, underwriting and disseminating custom program solutions.
